About us

GART 6520

Hello, we are GreenAms Robotics Team 6520. GreenAms 6520 Robotics Team is the first and only club and team from Hanoi-Amsterdam High School for the Gifted and Vietnam to participate FIRST Robotics Competition. We aim to build a STEM and Robotics-loving community through numerous public activities: Power Up Vietnam (co-organized with KidsCode), Tech Fair, GART Robotics Camp (under the patronage of The American Center Hanoi - The U.S. Embassy in Hanoi),...

Our events

GART EXPO - 2022

This was our first event in 2022. Coming out of pandemic, we faced a lot of problems but fortunately, GART EXPO was still a success for us.

FRC 2023

FRC 2023 is one of the biggest robotics competition for highschool students in the world. This year, we participated in the Southern Cross regional in Australia

GART TOUR

GART TOUR is an event of our club in which we teach STEM and robotics for secondary school students in Hanoi. This year, we have came to 3 schools which are Cao Ba Quat secondary school, Mai Dich secondary school and Hanoi Star Secondary school.

VRC 2023

VRC 2023 is one of the biggest robotics competition in Vietnam for highschool students. In this year, we have tried our best and passed the qualification rounds. Unfortunately, we are eliminated during the Playoffs of the competition
